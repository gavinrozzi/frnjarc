First Club Meeting - June 23rd, 2018
Published: June 08, 2018

The Forked River Amateur Radio Club will hold its first meeting on the morning of Saturday, June 23rd at 11:00 AM. Please bring your own radio equipment and folding chairs. We will discuss some of our goals for the club and how membmers will be able to participate going forward. The first meeting will largely be informal and a chance for members to get an idea of our vision for the club and what we hope to accomplish. [Read more from First Club Meeting - June 23rd, 2018...]
